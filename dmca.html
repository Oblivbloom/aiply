Copyright & DMCA Policy (EN)

Aiply (operated by SYNAPTIVE, hereinafter "we" or "Company") respects the rights of creators as our highest priority and strictly prohibits the infringement of others' intellectual property rights. As an Online Service Provider (OSP), we comply with global intellectual property laws, including the U.S. Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA, 17 U.S.C. § 512) and the Copyright Act of the Republic of Korea.

This policy defines clear procedures for responding promptly to claims of copyright infringement and protecting the rights of users whose content has been wrongfully removed.

1. How to Report Copyright Infringement (DMCA Takedown Notice)

If you believe that your copyrighted work (sound recording, lyrics, album art, etc.) or right of publicity (e.g., voice) has been used without authorization on the Aiply platform, please submit a written notice via email to our Designated Copyright Agent.

For a lawful and prompt response, your notice email must include the following 6 items required by law (DMCA § 512(c)(3)). If any item is missing, your notice may not be validly processed.

  1. Identification of the Original Work: A specific description of your original work claimed to have been infringed (e.g., original title, official release link).
  2. Location of the Infringing Material (URL Required): The specific sharing link (URL) or exact title and artist name of the material that is claimed to be infringing, so that we can accurately locate it on the Aiply platform.
  3. Contact Information of the Complaining Party: Your legal name, physical address including postal code, telephone number, and email address.
  4. Statement of Good Faith Belief: An explicit statement: "I have a good faith belief that use of the material in the manner complained of is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law."
  5. Statement Under Penalty of Perjury: An explicit statement: "The information in this notification is accurate, and under penalty of perjury, that I am authorized to act on behalf of the owner of an exclusive right that is allegedly infringed."
  6. Signature: A physical or electronic signature of the copyright owner or a person authorized to act on their behalf (typing your full legal name at the bottom of the email suffices as an electronic signature).

2. Counter-Notice for Removed Content

If you firmly believe that the content you uploaded to Aiply was wrongfully removed or disabled as a result of mistake or misidentification (e.g., you possess a valid commercial license for the beat, or your use qualifies as Fair Use), you may submit a Counter-Notice to us.

Your Counter-Notice email must include the following 4 items required by law (DMCA § 512(g)(3)).

  1. Identification of Removed Material: Identification of the material that has been removed or disabled and the location (URL) at which the material appeared on Aiply before it was removed.
  2. Statement Under Penalty of Perjury: A statement: "I state under penalty of perjury that I have a good faith belief that the material was removed or disabled as a result of mistake or misidentification of the material to be removed or disabled."
  3. Contact Info & Consent to Jurisdiction: Your legal name, physical address, telephone number, and email address. And a statement that "I consent to the jurisdiction of the Federal District Court for the judicial district in which my address is located (for US residents), or the Gwangju District Court of the Republic of Korea (for non-US residents), and that I will accept service of process from the person who provided the original infringement notification or an agent of such person."
  4. Signature: Your physical or electronic signature.

3. Repeat Infringer Policy & Legal Warnings

Aiply enforces a Zero Tolerance policy for the protection of creators' intellectual property rights. Content found to infringe on the rights of others (including copyrights and voice rights) will be immediately removed. Users who repeatedly commit such acts, or those found guilty of severe violations such as deepfakes (unauthorized voice cloning) (Repeat Infringers), will have their accounts permanently suspended without prior notice in accordance with our Community Guidelines.

🚨 Nullification of Contribution Metrics: If an account is permanently suspended, all platform activity metrics (Points and Notes) unjustly accumulated and any pending 'Creator and Activity Support Funds' associated with that account will be immediately nullified (reduced to 0).

⚠️ Warning Against False Claims and Counter-Notices

Under Section 512(f) of the US DMCA and the Copyright Act of the Republic of Korea, if you knowingly materially misrepresent that material is infringing, or conversely, falsely submit a counter-notice that it is not infringing, you may be subject to severe civil and criminal liability for damages incurred by us or other users, including attorneys' fees and court judgments. If you are unsure whether your rights have been clearly infringed, we strongly recommend seeking advice from a legal professional before submitting a notice.

  1. 侵害されたオリジナル著作物の特定: 侵害されたと主張するオリジナル著作物に関する具体的な説明（原作者の曲名、公式リリースリンク等）。

  3. 申告者の連絡先情報: 法的氏名、郵便番号を含む物理的住所、電話番号、およびメールアドレス。
  4. 誠実な信念の表明（Good Faith Belief）: 「私は、申告された方法での当該資料の使用が、著作権者、その代理人、または法律によって許可されていないと誠実に（good faith）確信しています。」という明示的な表明。
  5. 偽証罪の罰則を承知した上での正確性の表明（Penalty of Perjury）: 「本通知書の情報は正確であり、偽証罪の罰則を承知した上で、私が侵害されたと推定される独占的権利の所有者であるか、または所有者に代わって行動する権限があることを表明します。」という明示的な表明。
