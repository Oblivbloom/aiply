We collect the following data in a lawful and transparent manner to provide optimal services and enhance the AI-based user experience.

2.1. Information You Provide Directly or via Integration

  • Account & Profile Info: Name (or artist name), email address, profile picture, and cover image collected during social login (e.g., Google) and profile creation. (Note: We do not directly collect or store separate passwords or dates of birth.)
  • Device & App Activity Data: Music playback history (playback status, track info), acquired points and diamonds, artist ranking changes, blocked user lists, and minigame (Galaxy Smash) high scores.

  • Customer Support: Records of communication with us via customer service and profile features.

2.2. Information Collected Automatically

  • Device & Hardware Info: Unique device identifiers, OS version, Push Notification Identifier (FCM Token), and Session ID.
  • Usage & Log Data: Service access time (recent login and active time), traffic logs, app crash logs (Firebase Crashlytics), and system performance data (Firebase Analytics).
  • Location Info: Approximate geographic location inferred from your IP address. (We do not request permissions for or collect precise GPS-based location data.)

  • Social Media Integration (SSO): Verified identification information in accordance with the platform's policy and your settings when logging in via a Google account, etc.
