Aiply Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 10, 2026

English

1. Introduction

Aiply ("Company", "we", "our") values user privacy and data security. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you access or use the Aiply mobile application and related services.

This policy is designed to comply with major global data protection regulations including the Korean Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA), and the data safety policies of Google Play and Apple App Store.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account and profile information (artist name, email, profile image)
  • Music activity (play history, ranking changes, points, diamonds)
  • AI prompt inputs and generated results
  • Subscription and purchase information
  • Customer support communications

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information and OS version
  • App activity logs and crash reports
  • Session identifiers and notification tokens
  • Approximate location derived from IP address

3. Purpose of Data Use

  • Providing and operating services
  • AI interaction and result generation
  • Personalized user experience
  • Fraud prevention and platform security
  • Service updates and notifications
  • Legal compliance

4. Analytics and SDK

Aiply uses analytics technologies such as Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics to improve service performance and user experience. Users may control tracking settings through device privacy settings.

5. Data Processing and Third Parties

To provide stable services, certain data may be processed by trusted service providers.

  • Google LLC – Firebase, Cloud services, analytics
  • Google Play / Apple – in-app payment processing

6. Children's Privacy

Aiply does not intentionally collect information from children under the legal age defined by applicable laws.

7. User Rights and Account Deletion

Users may request access, correction, or deletion of their personal data at any time. Account deletion can be performed inside the app or by contacting support email.

8. Data Retention

Personal data is retained only while the account remains active and is deleted or anonymized upon account deletion unless retention is required by law.

9. Security

We implement strong encryption (TLS/SSL) and strict database security rules to prevent unauthorized access.

10. Policy Updates

Changes to this policy will be announced at least 7 days prior to implementation.
