Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2, 2026

Antigravity (hereinafter referred to as the "Company") values your privacy and complies with the Google Play Developer Policy and applicable laws. This Privacy Policy explains how Aiply collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Required Information: Email address, Login ID (UID), Nickname (Artist Name).
  • Optional Information: Profile image, Biography (Bio).
  • Generated Data: Uploaded audio files, album covers, chat messages (including AI interactions), transaction history (Points, Purchases), playback history, app usage logs, cookies, and device information.

2. Purpose of Collection and Use

  • Service Provision: User identification, music streaming/downloading, marketplace transactions, and premium membership management.
  • AI Services: Providing AI chatbot (AI Muse) features using the Google Gemini API. (Input text is used solely for generating AI responses.)
  • Service Improvement: Analyzing usage statistics using Google Firebase Analytics.

3. Third-Party Services and SDKs

The app uses the following third-party tools (SDKs) to provide services, which may transmit necessary data:

  • Google Firebase: Authentication, Database (Firestore), Storage, Analytics.
  • Google Gemini AI: Text processing for AI chat features.
  • YouTube Player API: Video content playback.

4. App Permissions

  • Internet (INTERNET): Server communication and data transmission.
  • Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS): Receiving service alerts.
  • Foreground Service (FOREGROUND_SERVICE): Maintaining background music playback.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Upon account deletion, your personal information and profile data stored on the server will be immediately deleted or anonymized. However, transaction records may be retained for a certain period as required by applicable laws.

6. Account Deletion and Contact (CRITICAL UPDATE)

You can request data deletion using one of the following methods:

  • In-App Deletion: Navigate to 'Settings > Delete Account' feature within the app for immediate processing.
  • Web-Based Request (If app access is unavailable): If you cannot access the app, you may request account deletion by sending an email to oblivbloom@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request". We will review and process your request within 7 days.
